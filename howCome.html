HOW TO COME

NEW ZEALAND

TRAVEL BY PLANE

Flying to New Zealand gives you the opportunity to enjoy breathtaking panoramic views from the plane, as well as the comfort of being able to rest during the long flight. You have the freedom to choose from a number of airlines and routes to find the option that best suits your needs. Once in New Zealand, you'll be able to enjoy a wide variety of outdoor activities and breathtaking scenery.

However, flights to New Zealand can be expensive, especially during peak season. Also, jet lag can affect your experience, as there is a big time difference between New Zealand and many other countries.

Despite the cons, if you're ready to embark on a unique adventure in one of the world's most breathtaking destinations, don't hesitate to plan your trip to New Zealand today!

TRAVEL BY BOAT

If you're looking for an alternative way to travel to New Zealand, consider travelling by boat. Travelling by boat provides a unique and relaxing experience, where you can enjoy the sea breeze and panoramic ocean views along the way. Some cruises even offer stopovers at different destinations, allowing you to explore other places before arriving in New Zealand. However, it's important to note that boat trips tend to be longer, which can affect the overall length of your trip. In addition, departure and arrival times may be less flexible and costs may be higher compared to flights. Carefully assess your preferences and needs before deciding if travelling by boat is the best option for your New Zealand adventure - be prepared to enjoy an unforgettable maritime experience and discover the wonders of this amazing destination!

FEATURED TRANSPORT IN NEW ZEALAND

Get ready for a thrilling experience in Wellington, New Zealand with the famous Wellington Cable Train! This iconic transport system will take you on an incredible journey through the city's hills, offering breathtaking panoramic views and a unique perspective of the natural beauty that surrounds the nation's capital. As the funicular glides gently down the steep slopes, you'll be able to admire Wellington's lush landscapes, picturesque buildings and beautiful harbour. Whether you're exploring the city or simply enjoying a scenic ride, the Wellington Cable Train is an unmissable experience that will give you lasting memories - be sure to hop on board and let yourself be swept away on this thrilling adventure to the top of Wellington!
