OUR PRINCIPAL OFFICES

New Zealand has three main tourism offices located in Auckland, Queenstown and Wellington. The main office is in Auckland, the country's largest and most cosmopolitan city, where you can get detailed information on tourist attractions, accommodation, restaurants and activities. On the South Island, the tourist office is in Queenstown, a city surrounded by mountains and lakes that offers a wide variety of outdoor activities. Finally, on the North Island, the tourist office is located in Wellington, New Zealand's capital city, famous for its vibrant cultural scene and beautiful natural harbour. At all three offices you can get detailed information on the main tourist attractions, as well as cultural events and festivals that take place throughout the year.

KNOW OUR OFFICES

AUCKLAND OFFICE

New Zealand's central tourism office in Auckland is a vibrant and welcoming place where visitors can get information and assistance in exploring the country. Located in the heart of the city, it offers a modern and energetic atmosphere. The highly trained staff is ready to provide personalised recommendations and helpful advice on destinations, activities and accommodation in New Zealand. In addition, the office features interactive displays and informative materials to help travellers plan their adventure. It is an ideal starting point to discover all that New Zealand has to offer.

WELLINGTON OFFICE

New Zealand's second tourist office in Wellington is a modern and welcoming place for visitors. Located in the city centre, it is staffed by knowledgeable and friendly people who are ready to provide detailed information on destinations and activities across the country. The office features interactive displays and information resources, making it the perfect place to start exploring the wonders of New Zealand. It also offers booking services and assistance to help visitors plan their trip. It is a must stop for travellers visiting Wellington.

QUEENSTOWN OFFICE

New Zealand's third tourist office in Queenstown is an impressive and modern venue for visitors. Located in the heart of the town, it features innovative architectural design and panoramic views of Queenstown's spectacular scenery. The highly trained staff are ready to provide detailed information on local attractions, outdoor activities and accommodation options. With interactive displays and booking services, this office is the perfect starting point for exploring the exciting adventures and natural beauty Queenstown has to offer - a must for nature lovers and outdoor sports enthusiasts!
